LUMS (Lahore University of Management Sciences) - Admission Information 2026

ABOUT LUMS
Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) is one of Pakistan's top-ranked private universities, located in Sector U, DHA, Lahore Cantt. LUMS is known for its merit-based, holistic admissions process and strong financial aid programmes, making quality education accessible to talented students from all socio-economic backgrounds.

SCHOOLS AND UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS
LUMS offers undergraduate programmes through four schools:
- Suleman Dawood School of Business (SDSB): BSc Accounting & Finance, BSc Management Science
- Mushtaq Ahmad Gurmani School of Humanities and Social Sciences (MGSHSS): English, History, Political Science, Psychology, Economics, Anthropology, Liberal Arts
- Syed Babar Ali School of Science and Engineering (SBASSE): Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics
- Shaikh Ahmad Hassan School of Law (SAHSOL): Five-year LLB programme

Each undergraduate programme at LUMS is a 4-year, 8-semester degree built on a liberal arts core, emphasizing critical thinking, leadership, and cross-disciplinary learning.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
Applicants must have completed (or be completing) one of the following:
- Matric with at least 70% marks AND FA (Part-1) with 65% marks or FSc (Part-1)/ICS (Part-1)/ICOM (Part-1) with 70% marks or above
- O-Level results (minimum 8 subjects) with an average grade of at least 'B' in all attempted subjects
- American High School Diploma (first year) with 70% or above / GPA of B or above
- International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (first year) with at least 28 out of 45 points
- A-Level candidates who do not meet minimum grade requirements may retake in November or June sessions, subject to specific eligibility windows

Diploma holders may apply if they have secured a minimum of 70% marks, along with an IBCC equivalence certificate.

ADMISSION TESTS
- LUMS accepts SAT, ACT, or the LUMS Common Admission Test (LCAT/LAT).
- LCAT Registration Fee: PKR 6,500 (if applicable).
- SAT College Code for reporting to LUMS: 0513
- ACT College Code for reporting to LUMS: 7896
- Best score from a single attempt is considered, even if the applicant has tested multiple times.
- There are specific validity windows for SAT/ACT scores depending on the admission cycle (Fall vs Spring).

HOW TO APPLY
1. Create an account on the LUMS Online Admissions portal (admission.lums.edu.pk)
2. Select the desired Degree Programme
3. Submit the completed Admission Form by the deadline in the Academic Calendar
4. Upload scanned copies of required supporting documents (transcripts, CNIC/B-Form, photographs, SAT/ACT scores if applicable)
5. Pay the Application Processing and Test Registration fees by the due date
6. Submit a Financial Aid application (if applicable) with supporting documents by the specified deadline
7. Ensure referees submit Teacher Evaluations or Letters of Recommendation (if required) by the deadline

Applicants who created an account with LUMS Online Admissions within the last two years do not need to create a new one and can use previous login credentials.

FEE STRUCTURE (Bachelors, FY 2026-27, approximate)
- Admission Fee: PKR 176,600 (one-time)
- Tuition Fee: PKR 732,800 per semester (Fall 2026 and Spring 2027)
- Total Tuition (32 credit hours @ PKR 45,800/credit): approximately PKR 1,465,600 per year
- Semester Registration Fee: PKR 56,100 per semester (~PKR 112,200/year)
- General Security Deposit: PKR 67,000 (one-time, refundable)
- Total Year 1 Fee (Humanities/Business/Law): approximately PKR 1,821,400 - 1,824,400
- Total Year 1 Fee (Science & Engineering/SBASSE): approximately PKR 2,182,200 (includes lab fee and extra credit hour)
- Note: Actual fees may differ slightly; refer to the official LUMS fee structure page for confirmation.

FINANCIAL AID AND SCHOLARSHIPS
LUMS offers one of Pakistan's strongest financial support systems:
- Need-based Financial Aid: up to full tuition waiver
- Merit Scholarships: awarded to top-performing students
- National Outreach Programme (NOP): full scholarship plus hostel accommodation for deserving students from underserved regions
- Special allowances for students from remote areas
- External scholarships such as Ehsaas Undergraduate Scholarship and HEC programmes are also accepted

Only applicants who indicate intention to apply for Financial Aid through the Online Admission Application, submit their application, and pay the processing fee by the deadline will gain access to the Financial Aid application tab.

TRANSFER ADMISSIONS
LUMS accepts transfer students from HEC-recognized institutions twice a year (before Fall and Spring semesters). Applicants must have completed at least one year of undergraduate study, with no more than one year of non-enrollment, before transferring to LUMS as a full-time student.

IMPORTANT NOTES
Meeting minimum eligibility criteria does not guarantee admission — LUMS admissions are competitive and merit-based, also considering diversity and leadership potential. Applications that do not meet minimum criteria will not be evaluated even if the applicant has taken the admission test.
