Riphah International University - Admission Information 2026

ABOUT RIPHAH INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITY
Riphah International University (RIU) is a chartered private university in Pakistan with multiple campuses including Islamabad, Lahore, Faisalabad, Malakand, and Gujranwala. Riphah integrates professional and technical education with Islamic ethical values, aiming to produce graduates who are technically proficient and socially responsible. The university offers BS, Masters (MA/MSc/MS/MPhil), and PhD programs across various faculties.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS (COMPUTING / IT)
Riphah Institute of Informatics (RII) and Riphah College of Science & Technology offer the following undergraduate computing programs:
- BS Computer Science (BSCS)
- BS Software Engineering (BSSE)
- BS Artificial Intelligence (BSAI)
- BS Information Technology (BSIT)
- BS Cyber Security (BSCSY)
- BS Data Science
- BS Computer Systems (with focus on AI, Data Science, Robotics, High-Performance Computing, and Computer Architecture)
- Bachelor in Computer Arts
- BS Computer Graphics and Visualization

BS COMPUTER SCIENCE (BSCS) PROGRAM DETAILS
The BSCS program provides a strong foundation in computing principles including programming, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, operating systems, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and database systems. The curriculum integrates Islamic ethical values and social responsibility within professional computing practice. Through hands-on projects, internships, and collaborative learning, graduates are prepared for careers as software engineers, system analysts, network administrators, application developers, data scientists, and cybersecurity specialists, or for higher studies in computing.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR BSCS AND RELATED PROGRAMS
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks in both SSC and HSSC (Intermediate), or equivalent qualification as per IBCC equivalence.
- HSSC must include Mathematics as a subject, or equivalent qualification certified by IBCC.
- Non-FA/FSc stream candidates must obtain an equivalence certificate from IBCC, Pakistan, in the relevant group/subjects.
- Pre-Medical applicants (Physics, Chemistry, Biology background) are eligible with at least 50% marks, but if admitted, they must study two additional Mathematics courses (6 credit hours total) during the first year. These deficiency courses are non-credited but appear on the student's final transcript.

FEE STRUCTURE (BSCS - approximate, varies by campus)
- Tuition Fee: approximately PKR 8,636 per credit hour (for 17 credit hour semester load) at some campuses; rates vary by campus and program (some campuses charge PKR 8,280 or PKR 5,321 per credit hour).
- Tuition, Exam, Enrollment, and Lab fees are charged each semester.
- Hostel charges (where applicable): approximately PKR 248,400 per semester for female boarders and PKR 216,000 per semester for male boarders.
- Fees exclude applicable taxes and levies.
- The university reserves the right to revise fees and course/credit hour offerings as deemed necessary.
- Fee structure listed is for local students only; fees for Overseas Pakistanis, Pakistanis holding foreign nationality, and international students differ and must be obtained directly from the University Admission Office.

ADMISSION PROCESS
- Riphah admissions are merit-based with transparent evaluation criteria.
- Riphah offers extensive financial assistance and scholarship programs to support deserving students from varied socio-economic backgrounds.
- Application process, eligibility criteria, and required documents are available through the official admissions portal at riphah.edu.pk/admissions.
- Enrollment deadlines vary by intake cycle (Fall/Spring); for example, a previous cycle's enrollment deadline was set for early February.
